    \begin{proof}[Solution of Problem 1]
        For a formal power series $f(x) = \sum_{i \ge 0} a_i x^i$, we define $\supp f(x) := \left\{ i \,;\, a_i \neq 0 \right\}}$, $\ord f(x) := \inf \supp f(x)$. 
    \end{proof}
